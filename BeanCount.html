🌿 Coffee 101

Coffee is more than just a drink — it's an agricultural science! There are different types of plantations like Arabica and Robusta, each having unique flavor and yield characteristics. Factors like altitude, rainfall, soil, and the farmer’s care can influence the yield and quality of coffee beans ☕.

This tool helps you estimate your potential yield based on key plantation details. Happy brewing! 🍃

🔍 About This Tool

The BeanCount Yield Estimator uses a simple rule-based model to predict potential coffee yield based on the following factors:

  • 🌾 Farm Size
  • 💧 Rainfall
  • ⛰️ Altitude
  • 🌱 Plantation Type
  • 🌿 Coffee Variety
  • 🪴 Plant Age & Health
  • 👩‍🌾 Farmer Care Practices

This model provides a basic estimate and does not replace detailed agronomic analysis.
